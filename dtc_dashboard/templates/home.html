DTC DASHBOARD

To provide an efficient web application or dashboard for the public and government agencies, to monitor the DTC buses in realtime and to get the congestion details.

Source: https://www.pinterest.com/pin/360780620131576373/

LIVE TRACKING

The page shows the cluster of buses running at that time and can track the real-time movement of a bus. The live tracking of a bus movement shows the line in different colors, which signifies the different levels of congestion on the route.

BUS PLAYBACK

The page is used to get the playback of a particular bus i.e to see the movement of the bus from the past recorded data. The playback is helpful in finding the type of data transmitted by the sensors and can find different stats like congestion on a particular route.

DOWNLOAD

The page helps the users to download the bus data in CSV format for a time period of one day for the date given in input. The data download is based on the vehicle id or the route id.

ABOUT US

The page is used to show information about the project guide and the developers of the website. The page contains the links to the email and linked in handels and also contains the contact information.
